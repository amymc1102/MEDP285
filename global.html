Providing fresh affordable produce to all New Yorkers.

Global Greens NYC aims to serve all New Yorkers with information of affordable farmers market and free community programs that provides fresh fruits and vegetables. We believe that all New Yorkers and their families regardless of their socio-economic income deserves to have access to healthy produce without having to spend a substanial amount of money. Due to covid-19, many families are without food and jobs. Information and access to food programs and resources are important during these times, so we can make sure that no New Yorkers go hungry.

Under the information section is an interactive map that shows free/affordable farmers market and community fridges within the five boroughs of New York City. All of the farmers market listed accept EBT cards.
