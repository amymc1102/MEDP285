The Machine Stops Reflection:

The Machine Stops by E.M Forester tells a story of a dystopian world in which everything is connected and rule by the machine. The machine reflects the technology that is ever so present throughout our life. In fact, in our modern time, humans also depend on technology to communicate and interact with each other more than ever than before.
