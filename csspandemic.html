Covid-19 Pandemic Timeline in the United States/New York 2020

Link to my article reference

First Reported Case of Covid-19 Case in Washington State.

March 6: 21 Passengers on California Cruise Ship Test Positive.
March 13 WHO/Trump Declares Covid-19 a national Emergency. Travel Ban on Non-US Citizens traveling from Europe.
March 14: First two Covid-19 deaths in NYS. March 16: NYC Public Schools Close. March 17: NYC bars & restaurants close for dine in. March 22: NYS pause program begins. March 27: Trump signs CARES Act.
April 10:NYS has highest Covid cases. April 15: Face masks required. April 30, Gov.Cuomo closes NYC subway from 1 a.m to 5 a.m. May 1: All schools closed for remainder of year.
June 8: NYC begins Phase 1 reopening, June 22: NYC begins Phase 2 reopening. U.S Covid-19 deaths surpass 130,000. July 6: Phase 3 reopening, July 19: Phase 4 reopening without indoor dining/bars.
September 2: Gyms reopen, September 9: Malls reopen in NYC with 50% capacity, September 29, Elementary students return to Public School Classrooms in NYC. September 30:Indoor dining in NYC resumes with 25% capacity.
October 2: President and first lady Melania Trump test positive for Covid-19.
October 4-5: Mayor De Blasio will close 9 zip codes in Brooklyn and Queens if approved.
